Privacy Policy

At Albatross, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information Collection

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, shipping details, and payment information when you register or place an order.

2. Use of Information

Your information is used to process orders, provide customer service, personalize your experience, and send relevant promotional offers.

3. Data Protection

We implement strict security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

4. Sharing Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Your information may only be shared with trusted third-party service providers to fulfill orders or improve services.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience. Cookies help track preferences, shopping cart content, and website analytics.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information. Contact our support team for assistance.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Users are encouraged to review it regularly for any changes.
